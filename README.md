The SQL Pizza Sales Analysis project is designed to delve into the patterns, trends, and insights hidden within a pizza store's sales data. By leveraging SQL's powerful querying capabilities, this project aims to comprehensively understand customer preferences, sales performance, and operational trends that can drive data-driven decision-making.

In this project, you will explore various aspects of the sales data, such as identifying the most popular pizza types, analyzing revenue generation, and uncovering ordering patterns across different times and categories. Key areas of focus in this project include:

Category-Wise Analysis: Examining the distribution of orders across different pizza categories, helping to understand customer preferences and adjust the menu offerings accordingly.
